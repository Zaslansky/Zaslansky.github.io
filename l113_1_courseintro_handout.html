Building Our Culture of Integrity

I have three starter rules about integrity this quarter

  • Your discussions should be completed by you, in your own words and show your own knowledge

  • All sources external to the class should be cited in a way that we can find the reference

  • Please show integrity asking for exceptions and grade changes
